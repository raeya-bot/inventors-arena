Our Experts

Meet Edwin & Scott

Two seasoned experts who bring the patent, engineering, and business sides together, guiding independent inventors from a raw idea to a funded, market-ready product.

Edwin Wold

Co-Founder & Invention Strategist US Patent Agent

Edwin Wold is a seasoned US Patent Agent. He enjoys helping independent inventors and small companies develop their invention ideas to fruition. Throughout his career, Edwin noted how inventors often struggle with the development of their products and obtaining the investment funding needed to help their products to market. Edwin teamed up with Scott to bring both the patent and business sides together in a holistic program, teaching inventors the skills needed for their success.

Scott Romeo

Co-Founder & Investor Relations Certified Business Negotiator Wharton M&A Certificate 32+ Years Experience

Scott Romeo is a business and management consultant with 32 years' experience in nearly 90 industries. His strength is in developing the idea of an inventor into a viable business offering. He writes strategic business plans and conducts due diligence on business plans for investors. He is a Certified Business Negotiator and holds a certificate in Mergers and Acquisitions from Wharton. He provides the business side to complement Edwin, providing the patent and engineering know-how.
